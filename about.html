Who We Are

about Elijah Edifice

We are a CAC registered Nigerian real estate company dedicated to helping Nigerians find and own genuine land and properties across the country.


With over 5 years of experience in the Nigerian real estate market, we have helped more than 500 clients successfully purchase verified land and properties. We understand the fears and challenges Nigerians face when buying property — fake listings, fraudulent agents and fake documents. That is why we built Elijah Edifice on a foundation of trust and transparency.


Every property we list is physically inspected and legally verified. We work with qualified lawyers and surveyors to ensure every transaction is genuine and every client walks away with proper documentation.
